Not sure what comes after school? Let's figure it out.

Pathfinder Edu is a small set of free AI guides for students finishing high school — to explore careers, choose colleges, plan how to pay, and write applications. No app, no sign-up. They work in any AI chat you already have.

Explore the guides How it works
Three simple steps

It's just a conversation. Here's how.

Every guide is plain text — nothing to install, nothing to pay. If you can open a chat, you can use it.

1

Pick a guide & copy it

Choose a guide below and press Copy prompt. The whole tool is just a block of text.

2

Open any AI chat

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot — any of them work. No special account, no setup tricks.

3

Paste & talk honestly

Paste the prompt, say "hi", and answer like yourself. There are no wrong answers.
